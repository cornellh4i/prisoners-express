**“Both Body and Soul” by Leroy Sodorff**

A chain of events

both body and soul

tethered together

in the middle of the road.

While the body gets the distance,

and carries the load,

the eyes for the body,

both triumphant and bold,

share a common bond

as they cross the threshold.

***A Do* by LeRoy Sodorff**

cut

perm

and spit curl

finger wave, feather

and coiffure.

coif

pageboy

and shag

pigtail, puff

and bang.

pixie cut

ponytail

and braid

cornrow, pin curl

and plait.

To witness

this process

is really hair-raising,

but each emerging being

is truly amazing.

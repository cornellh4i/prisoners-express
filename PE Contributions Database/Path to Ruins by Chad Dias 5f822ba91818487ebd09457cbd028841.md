**"Path to Ruins" by Chad Dias**

Smoked weed, popped E’s, drank booze and smoked cigs under the pretense that these were just some of the things cool guys normally did. Simply trying to keep up with whatever was in. No time for wasting, we’re getting wasted until our speech is incoherent, and we’re having trouble remembering exactly what we did. So what if we’re kids? This is just what young people casually did. LSD, shrooms, heroin, and cocaine are still in. Crystal meth and prescription drugs, the syrup will give you a buzz. But again, who can blame us if addiction were to change us from the loving children you once knew into strangers whose primary pursuit is chasing a high| I mean we’re simply trying to be like the cool guys. Yet if that were true, why do you have to hurt or lose the people who love you in order to pursue it? Why do you have to hide the fact that you do it? Better yet, why if it’s so favorable to pursue, do people consider such a pursuit the path to ruins...
